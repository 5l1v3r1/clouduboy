Clouduboy is a free & open-source online Arduboy Game Editor + Arduino IDE. For more info, refer to the README.

Don't have a session id yet? You can create one below:

?-?-?

A session ID is used to identify you across all your devices. Open Clouduboy on your phone, or tablet and type your session ID to interact with your edited project simultaneously on any of your devices.

Note: your sessions will be deleted after you haven't been using them for a while.

Flash your games directly from your browser!

To fully enjoy Clouduboy, you may download the Clouduboy Flasher to your computer. With the Flasher, will be able to upload your games & sketches to your Arduboy/Arduino, without ever needing to leave the editor.
